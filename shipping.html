Shipping

1.How much is Shipping?

Shipping depends on the quantity of products ordered and the delivery place. The less products and the closer the delivery place, the lower the shipping will be.

2.How long will the delivery take?

Orders in the North will take between 2 to 5 days, orders in the Center will take between 5 to 8 days and orders in the South will take between 7 to 12 days. (All these deadlines are estimated, orders may take longer due to several factors)

3.Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, we deliver in any country, however, international deliveries have higher freight and a longer delivery period.

4.Can I get free shipping in some conditions?

You may be entitled to free shipping if you make a purchase with a value greater than 2000 € or if there is a current promotion that gives you this right.

5.Why haven't I recieved my product yet?

That depends on several factors. There may have been disruptions during the trip or it may take longer due to a high number of orders. However, if two weeks of estimated delivery time have not yet received your product, please contact us so we can solve your problem.
